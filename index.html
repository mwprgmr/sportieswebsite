About the park

A complete day-out destination for families, teams and communities.

Mission

To create a safe, energetic and welcoming place where children, friends, families and teams can play, celebrate, relax and build lasting memories.

Vision

To become the region's most loved recreational campus by continuously adding thoughtful facilities, modern games and warm hospitality.

Open Hours

Daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM for play zones, cafe service, events and selected wellness appointments.
